Black Cats

Black cats are less likely to be adopted form shelters in the US becuase it is believed that black cats are a symbol of bad omens, specifically of being the familiars of witches, or actually shape-shifting witches themselves. Most of Europe considers the black cat a symbol of bad luck, particularly if one walks across the path in front of a person, which is believed to be an omen of misfortune and death.

Bengal Cats

After generations of breeding the Bengal Cat has gotten the temperment of a normal houe cat, but the early generations ar mainly reserved for breeding. Bengals are known to like water and require a bug amount of attention to keep then happy. They also tend to be more "talkative" than most cat breeds.

Snowshoe Cats

The Snowshoe is a rare type of cat due to the difficulty of breeding cats with markings and patterns that go well with the breeding standards. The body type complicates the breeding because they need to have the right body and ear set. The personalities of Snowshoe cats are that they are usually affectionate, have a good temperament, and are mellow.
